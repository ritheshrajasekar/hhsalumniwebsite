How to Search

  • On the home page, you can search for Hightstown High School Alumni.
  • You can search for alumni using different key words such as first name, last name, college name, HHS graduation year, areas of interests, career fields, and more!

How to Add Your Profile

  • If you are a Hightstown High School Alumni, we encourage you to add your profile on the Add Profile page!
  • Once you submit your profile, it will be approved by an administrator before your profile will become public.

How to Update Your Profile

  • To update your current profile, please send an email to hightstownstudentcouncil@gmail.com detailing what you would like to change.
  • Note: All updates must be sent from the email that the profile was created with

Additional Questions

  • For any other questions or comments, please reach out to us at hightstownstudentcouncil@gmail.com
