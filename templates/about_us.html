Our Mission

At Hightstown High School, our sense of community is extremely invaluable. We know that the relationships we’ve built doesn’t have to end at graduation, and through our HHS Alumni page, we aim to help nourish these connections for a lifetime. To us, a community is more than belonging. Our goal is to go one step further and make belonging matter. Feel free to reach out to old and new friends alike. As Rams, we’re strong. But together as a herd, we’re stronger. Let’s stay connected!
